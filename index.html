Blog Post Sample
The Clean Content Framework
About This Sample
1,623-word strategic blog post on why minimalist copy silently outsells high-noise marketing. Includes a case study, stat block, pull quotes, objection handling, and a 3-step action plan.
Opening Hook
"You added more. More bullet points. More adjectives. More offers crammed onto one page. And somehow, the more you said, the less anyone listened. Here's the counterintuitive truth that took me years to fully accept: clutter isn't a design problem. It's a trust problem."
The Framework
1. The One-Breath Test — Can you explain what this page offers in a single breath? 2. The Stranger Test — Show your homepage to someone who's never heard of your brand. What do they see? 3. The "So What?" Filter — Does every sentence teach, persuade, or advance the narrative? 4. The One-Door Rule — Each page should have exactly one primary action.
